Our Specialist Services

Process & Hygienic Pipework

Expert design, fabrication, and installation of stainless steel and carbon steel pipework systems. Specialising in hygienic/dairy pipework for the food and beverage industry, ensuring compliance with strict standards. Also providing process pipework solutions for various industrial applications.

Stainless Steel Fabrication

Bespoke fabrication of stainless steel components, structures, tanks, platforms, and guards. High-quality TIG welding for durable and clean finishes suitable for food production, pharmaceutical, and architectural applications.

Structural Welding & Site Work

Providing certified structural welding services (MIG, Stick) for the construction industry, including beams, columns, and framework. Fully equipped for on-site welding, installation, modification, and repair work across North Wales and Chester.
